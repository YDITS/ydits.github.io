Quickly check disaster prevention information

Displays the current earthquake early warning (EEW), expected seismic intensity and countdown* of EEW in the current location, history of the most recent earthquake information, expected typhoon path map, and rain cloud radar.

In order to provide disaster prevention information promptly, this app does not contain any advertisements.

*The expected seismic intensity and countdown is displayed when the district forecast area for the current location is subject to a warning. You must allow location permission, if you use this feature.
*The expected seismic intensity and the countdown of the expected arrival of the main motion are the information released for the district forecast area of the current location.

Support for Shaking Preparedness

While this app is open, an audio notification will be provided when an EEW or earthquake information is announced. When the earthquake early warning is issued for your current location, a pop-up window will also notify you, support you prepare for the shaking.
